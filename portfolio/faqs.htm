Frequently Asked Questions

What does "ibamba" mean?

"Ibamba" is the Punu word for "white man." The Bapunu live in southern Gabon, in central Africa, where I spent two years teaching math.

How is it pronounced?

ee-BOMB-ah

What's up with the angry man?

He just looks angry. Once you get to know him he's very pleasant.

Does he dance?

Yes. Glad you asked.

If six people sitting around a table toast each other, and each person clinks glasses once with every other person, how many distinct clinks will there be?

Fifteen. The answer is the number of combinations of six items taken two at a time:

6C2 = 6! / [2(6-2)!] = 15
