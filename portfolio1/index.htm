 www.millenniumassessment.org

The Millennium Ecosystem Assessment is an analysis of the capacity of the world’s ecosystems to provide goods and services important for human development. The project’s website will act as a central repository for data gathered by the assessment on the condition of ecosystems around the world, while also providing timely information on the status and methodology of the assessment. This site has just been given a clean, simple, and easily navigable new design by ibamba.net.
