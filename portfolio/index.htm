Millennium Ecosystem Assessment

The Millennium Ecosystem Assessment (MA) is an analysis of the capacity of the world's ecosystems to provide goods and services important for human development. The project's website will act as a central repository for data gathered by the assessment on the condition of ecosystems around the world, while also providing timely information on the status and methodology of the assessment.

The site was redesigned to coincide with the publishing of the assessment's first product, "Ecosystems and Human-Wellbeing". The redesigned site is more flexible and dynamic, and is centered around the results that the assessment is beginning to generate. The site now features a full range of content-management tools, allowing staff around the world to enter news updates and upload documents through a simple web interface.

millenniumassessment.org

World Bank: Africa Regional Program on Enterprise Development

The Africa RPED is an ongoing economic research project that is developing business knowledge and policy advice for private-sector manufacturing development in Sub-Saharan Africa. The website includes a searchable online document library of research documents generated by the project. 

The site features management tools that allow RPED staff to upload, label, and organize documents.

Along with the website, ibamba.net developed a new logo and graphic identity for RPED.

BrownfieldSource

BrownfieldSource, a project of ICMA, is a central clearinghouse for brownfields redevelopment information. It features news articles, a calendar of events, a document library, and a directory of contractors. It can be browsed by topic as well; topics include economics, government, innovation, and community.

This site was designed by ibamba.net for development by another vendor, which built the back-end applications.

brownfieldsource.org



Pathways

Pathways is a project of the World Resources Institute's Sustainable Enterprise Program. It provides a repository of downloadable PowerPoint presentations compiling current knowledge about the whys and hows of sustainable business.

The site provides tools for business people who wish to make the case for responsible practices within their organizations.

pathways.wri.org


Millennium Assessment Intranet

This collaborative website allows teams of scientists researchers, and administrators all over the world to collaborate on one of the largest environmental research projects in history. It includes a document library for file sharing with a flexible and secure system of access permissions; a travel board; a fully-featured contacts database; email list management tools; and a catalog of common data sources.

We designed and built this site using ASP.NET and SQL Server technology.





 

American Architectural Foundation

The American Architectural Foundation is dedicated to helping people understand the importance of architecture in their lives. The newly redesigned AAF website reflects this emphasis on thoughtful, human-oriented design in its spare, user-friendly layout. The site is filled with striking architectural photographs from the AAF's extensive library of images.

The redesign includes sites for the Octagon Museum (the oldest museum in the United States dedicated to architecture) and the Mayors' Institute for City Design. These sister sites share the same visual approach while retaining a distinct identity.

archfoundation.org


Global Forest Watch

Global Forest Watch, a project of the World Resources Institute, is an international data and mapping network that combines on-the-ground knowledge with digital technology to provide accurate information about the world's forests.

The redesigned GFW site not only has a fresh, uncluttered graphic look, but has been streamlined and reorganized to eliminate obstacles separating the user from the data (maps, charts, photos, and reports).

globalforestwatch.org

ICMA Intranet

ICMA (International City/County Management Association) is a professional and educational organization representing appointed managers and administrators in local governments throughout the world.

The association's intranet, inside.icma.org, provides staff with easy access to existing databases, such as the membership directory. It also automates many routine office tasks for the first time (including equipment checkout, supply ordering, and staff travel & leave notices) and allows staff to post public announcements.

ibamba.net designed built this site using ASP and SQL Server.
